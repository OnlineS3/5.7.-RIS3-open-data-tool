Using the Application

Walkthrough individual use-cases of the RIS3 Open Data Tool as a worked example.

  1. Search
  2. Comparative Views: Group By

Search

Search for projects concerning a particular topic of interest. Search terms can be related to project: title, objectives, funding providers, regions and many more.

Step 1: Define a search term concerning one of the following properties of the projects in interest and select filters to refine the search further to narrow down on projects of interest implemented in a particular region:

  • Key Term: A topic of interest e.g. RIS3
  • Industry: A classification of project Business Area
  • Region: Area of implementation


Step 1: Defining a search term and applying optional filters.


Step 2: Explore the search results concerning projects and participating organisations grouped by region. Analyse the in-depth results and make comparisons between regions and rankings.
a: Analyse the result in the form of a Project Heatmap/Gantt chart displaying the timespan and distribution of projects implemented across regions. More dense lines represent a higher concentration of projects funded in a particular region.


Step 2 (a): Project Heatmap Analysis.


Step 2: Explore the search results concerning projects and participating organisations grouped by region. Analyse the in-depth results and make comparisons between regions and rankings.
b: Get an in-depth breakdown of the projects implemented in each region.


Step 2 (b): Results grouped by Region.


Step 2: Explore the search results concerning projects and participating organisations grouped by region. Analyse the in-depth results and make comparisons between regions and rankings.
c: Visualise the geographical spread of the organisations implementing these projects.


Step 2 (c): Explore the map of organisations
